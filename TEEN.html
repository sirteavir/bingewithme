The Outer Banks....
If you are looking for a feel-good teen drama that allows your adrenaline rush to go crazy. This is the one to binge to honestly. The drama constitutes of four friends who set out in an adventure to find the millions of cash lost in the Royal Merchant that sunk a couple of years ago.
Join John B, Kiara , JJ and Pope in this exciting mission as they discover the adventures the world holds for them.
